The PlayStation 2 (PS2) is the best-selling video game console of all time, with over 155 million units sold worldwide. Released in 2000, the PS2 dominated the gaming landscape for over a decade with its massive library of over 4,000 games. From groundbreaking titles like Shadow of the Colossus to genre-defining series like Grand Theft Auto, the PS2 era represents a golden age of gaming innovation.
