PlayStation 3

The PlayStation 3 (PS3) revolutionized gaming with its powerful Cell processor and Blu-ray capabilities. Released in 2006, the PS3 introduced gamers to HD gaming and a rich library of exclusive titles that are still beloved today. With its innovative DualShock 3 controller and free online multiplayer, the PS3 remains a favorite among gaming enthusiasts.

  • Cell Broadband Engine CPU
  • 256MB XDR RAM
  • 20GB-500GB Storage options
  • Built-in Blu-ray player
  • DualShock 3 controller with motion sensing
